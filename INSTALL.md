# Install Pocket Lighthouse on your phone

Play offline from your home screen — no App Store required.

**Live game:** https://aamorin.github.io/pocket-lighthouse/

---

## iPhone / iPad

1. Open the link above in **Safari** (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow at the bottom of the screen)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

The game icon appears on your home screen. Open it once on Wi-Fi to cache everything, then it works fully offline.

---

## Android

1. Open the link above in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **⋮ menu** (top right)
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** (or "Install app" on newer Chrome)
4. Tap **Add**

---

## Troubleshooting

- **iPhone: "Add to Home Screen" is missing** — you must use Safari, not an in-app browser or Chrome.
- **Game doesn't load offline** — open it once on Wi-Fi first so the service worker can cache the files.
- **Icon shows a screenshot instead of the lighthouse** — wait a few seconds after installing; iOS sometimes takes a moment to apply the icon.
